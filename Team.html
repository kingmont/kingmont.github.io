Associate - Peter Senior, B.S     
Peter holds a degree in Computer Science and Mathematics from York University; additionally, completed his studies in the Assessment Practice and Principles program at Queens University. Upon graduation, Peter worked for the Ministry of Finance for almost 27 years, practically writing the book on property assessments and valuations, before leaving and venturing out on his own in 1998.  As a freelancer, Peter’s services have been highly sought after by many industry firms; happily however choosing to share most of his expertise and time with the Kingmont Consulting team.
