The Property Tax Experts

Kingmont Consulting, Professional Corporation is a full service assessment and property tax consulting firm that specializes in providing taxpayers of commercial, industrial and special purpose properties with full-range property assessment and tax management services.

Our Mission

"Providing our valued customers with small firm customer service and familiarity, in partnership with large firm experience and expertise."

The Kingmont Consulting Advantage

Kingmont is committed to ensuring that any property tax savings achieved on behalf of our clients are not simply replaced by the consulting and litigation costs incurred to achieve the tax savings.

Unlike many of our competitors, we do not pay our staff based on the quantity of their billings. Rather, we are focused on sustaining long-term relationships with our clients, which we believe is contingent upon their receipt of fees that are both justified and cost effective.

Our Experience

Kingmont principals and staff have well over a century of combined experience in the ever changing legislative landscape of current value assessment and municipal taxation. On the tax calculation side, our team has lived and flourished surrounded by the fluid nature of the property taxation rules which continue to follow the many incarnations of the Assessment Act and the Municipal Act.

About Us

Kingmont was founded in 1988 by Edward Ford-King and David Montgomery. After years at the Ministry of Finance and the regional municipal level, they decided it was time for a change. Today, Kingmont has a team of associates who provide their valued customers with small firm customer service and familiarity, in partnership with large firm experience and expertise. This has allowed Kingmont to become one of the most respected property tax consulting firms in Canada today.

Kingmont's growth strategy over the years has been simple; attract only experienced and highly dedicated associates who share our core values and work ethics. This has allowed Kingmont to grow through word of mouth recommendations from their existing client base. Further, Kingmont's success can be directly attributed to our commitment to provide an exceptional customer service experience for our clients.

Kingmont has made it our business to keep abreast of the changes in legislation and the administration of municipal taxation. All of our associates, including the principals, are regularly involved in seminars and industry sponsored events. Additionally, we are required to attend yearly Continuing Professional Development classes provided by the Law Society of Upper Canada.

Services

Kingmont provides consultation and advocacy services to owners and tenants of commercial, industrial and special purpose properties with full-range property assessment and tax management services.

  • Assessment Appeals, Requests for Reconsideration
  • Management of Tax and Apportionments
  • Budget Estimates and Tax Allocation
  • Vacancy Rebate Applications
  • Property Assessment Change Notices – Reviews and Appeals
  • Municipal Act Applications and Appeals

Property Assessments received by taxpayers should be reviewed every year, both for accuracy and fairness.

Appealing your Assessment

Kingmont will file an appeal to the Assessment Review Board for non-residential properties or a Request for Reconsideration to the Municipal Assessment Corporation. A provincial filing fee is required for all appeals to the Assessment Review Board. Appeals must be filed to the Assessment Review Board no later than March 31st. Property Assessment Change appeals have staggered appeal deadlines based on the Notice mailing date.

Recovery of taxes

In order to recover property taxes previously paid due to vacancy, demolition, fire or other causes, an application under the Municipal Act must be filed to the municipality.
