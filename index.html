The Property Tax Experts

Kingmont Consulting is a full service property tax consulting firm that specializes in providing taxpayers of commercial, industrial, multi-residential and special purpose properties with full-range property assessment and tax management services.

Mission

“Providing our valued customers with small firm customer service and familiarity, in partnership with large firm experience and expertise”

Kingmont Consulting Advantage

Kingmont's resolution is to ensure that any property tax savings achieved on behalf of our clients are not simply replaced by the consulting and litigation costs incurred to achieve the tax savings strived for, which presently seems to be the case throughout our industry these days.

Unlike our competitors, we do NOT pay our staff based on the quantity of their billings. Rather, we are interested in sustaining a long-term relationship with our clients, which we believe in contingent upon their receipt of fees that are both justified, and at the same time, cost effective.

Experience

Kingmont principles and staff have well over a century of combined experience in the ever changing legislative landscape of “Current Value Assessment”. On the tax calculation side, our team has lived and flourished surrounded by the fluid nature of the property taxation rules which continue to follow the many incarnations of the Municipal Act.
