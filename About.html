Kingmont founders, Edward Ford-King and David Montgomery, had attained well over 40 years of valuable experience in their respective fields of assessment and property taxations at the Ministry of Finance and at the regional municipal level before joining forces and establishing their own firm in 1988. Today, Kingmont has a team of just under 10 associates with well over a century of combined industry experience; providing their valued customers with small firm customer service and familiarity, in partnership with large firm experience and expertise, allowing the company to become one of the most respected property tax consulting firms in Canada today. 


Kingmont’s growth strategy over the years has been simple; bring in only experienced and highly dedicated associates whom share their own core values and work ethics. This simple stratagem has allowed Kingmont to grow almost exclusively through word of mouth recommendations from their existing client base. Further, the company’s success can be directly attributed to the firm’s commitment of providing an exceptional customer service experience for their clients. 


Kingmont has made it their business to keep abreast of the changes in legislation and the administration of municipal taxations. All of their associates, including the principles are regularly involved in seminars and industry sponsored events; additionally, are required to attend yearly Continuing Professional Development classes provided by the Law Society of Upper Canada.
